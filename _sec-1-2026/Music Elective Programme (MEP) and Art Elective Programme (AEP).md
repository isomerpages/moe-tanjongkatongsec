<p>If your child/ ward exhibits interest and passion for Art or Music, they
can apply for Art Elective Programme (AEP) or Music Elective Programme
(MEP) and lessons will be conducted in the following centres:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>AEP Centre (National Junior College)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>MEP Centres (Anglo-Chinese School (Independent) or Dunman High School)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Successful applicants will attend weekly lessons in the afternoon at the
AEP/MEP Centres. In view of the extended Art/Music curriculum, AEP/MEP
students will not be offered Design and Technology (D&amp;T) and Food and
Consumer Education (FCE) at the Lower Secondary level to ensure that their
overall curriculum load is manageable.</p>
<p>The online application is usually done at the beginning of each academic
year.</p>

</p>
