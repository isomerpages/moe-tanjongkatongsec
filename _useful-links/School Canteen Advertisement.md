<h2>School Canteen Advertisement</h2>
<h4><strong>Indian Cuisine Stall (Halal / Non Halal) (Secondary School)</strong></h4>
<p>The School invites interested and suitably qualified vendors to apply
for the operation of an <strong>Indian Cuisine stall</strong> in our secondary
school canteen.</p>
<p><strong>Preferred Cuisine</strong>
</p>
<p>Indian Cuisine (North and/or South Indian)
<br>Both Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian options are required.</p>
<p>Vendors are encouraged to offer a variety of balanced meal sets suitable
for teenagers, taking into consideration portion adequacy, nutrition, and
affordability.</p>
<p><strong>Scope of Service</strong>
</p>
<p>The appointed vendor will be required to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Provide daily balanced set meals in accordance with the <strong>MOE Healthy Meals in Schools Programme (HMSP)</strong> guidelines</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Offer wholegrain options such as brown rice and whole meal products</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Include a minimum of two servings of vegetables per meal</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide fresh fruit daily as part of set meals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encouraged to use healthier cooking methods (e.g. steaming, baking, grilling,
stewing; no deep-fried main dishes)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure food is prepared with reduced oil, sugar, and sodium</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain student-friendly pricing appropriate for secondary school students</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Operational Expectations (Secondary School Context)</strong>
</p>
<p>The vendor must:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Operate during recess, lunch periods and till 3.30pm on school CCA Days</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be able to manage higher volume demand during peak times</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure efficient service to minimise waiting time</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain consistent food quality and portion control</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be prepared to adjust menu offerings based on student feedback (with school
approval)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Eligibility Criteria</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Stallholder(s) and assistant(s) who are Singapore Citizens or Singapore
Permanent Residents, aged 21 years old and above.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hold or be eligible to obtain a valid SFA Food Shop Licence</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure all food handlers possess valid Food Safety Certification</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Both applicant and stall assistant/s must register and pass the WSQ Food
safety course Level 1 conducted by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG) accredited
training providers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Successful applicant will need to apply for a food stall license from
Singapore Food Agency (SFA).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applicant must be in personal attendance at the stall during canteen operating
hours.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to comply with the latest Healthy Meals in Schools Programme (HMSP)
guidelines. For more details please refer to <a href="https://www.hpb.gov.sg/docs/default-source/default-document-library/healthy-meals-in-school-programme-v2-0-guidelines_final88c1ad7eb0824d0ca7e4a4be73092659.pdf?sfvrsn=9c43e02b_0" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">HPB's website</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have relevant experience in food stall or catering operations (preferred)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate commitment to hygiene, punctuality, and reliability</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Evaluation Criteria</strong>
</p>
<p>Applications will be assessed based on:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Compliance with HMSP nutritional guidelines</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Menu variety and suitability for teenagers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pricing and affordability</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Vendor experience and track record</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Food tasting (if shortlisted)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Interview performance</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The School reserves the right to reject any application without assigning
any reason.</p>

</p>
<p>Interested applicants are to submit:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Download and complete the <a href="/files/FormBF7_canteen_application_form.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">application form</a>.
You may also request for a hardcopy from the School’s General Office</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Photocopy of NRIC (Front and back)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proposed Healthy Set Meal Menu and Price list of each set meal size</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Brief profile and experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Copies of relevant licences and certifications (Basic Food Hygiene Certificate
and Certificate of Participation for Culinary Training Workshop for HMSP
by HPB (if any)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contact details</p>
</li>
</ul>
