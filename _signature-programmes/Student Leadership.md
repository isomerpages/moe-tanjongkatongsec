<h1>Student Leadership</h1>
<p><strong>Leadership Philosophy</strong>
</p>
<p>&nbsp;We believe that every TK student can and will rise to the occasion
to take the lead. We strive to be an extraordinary school that nurtures
leaders who will contribute to our school, nation and the global community.</p>
<p><strong>The Leadership Challenge Model</strong>
</p>
<p>&nbsp;TK Student Leadership framework is derived from the theoretical
underpinnings of The Leadership Challenge Model by Kouzes and Posner. The
Five Practices of The Leadership Challenge Model are:</p>

<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Signature%20Programmes/Student%20Leadership/leadership-challenge-model.jpg">
</div>

</p>

<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Signature%20Programmes/Student%20Leadership/framework.png">
</div>
<p><strong>3 Facets of Leadership</strong>
</p>
<p>&nbsp;The&nbsp;<strong>Competent</strong>&nbsp;facet aims to enable leaders
to manage their soft skills as leaders which include, vision casting, rallying
people through setting up clear aims and running projects, managing and
ensuring the efficient execution of events/tasks.</p>
<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Constructive</strong>&nbsp;facet focuses on value adding
to self and others in a position of leadership. Leaders are to grow in
awareness of self and schoolmates. By heightening their personal awareness
and empowering to positively influence both peers and juniors, they will
grow as active contributors to create a positive and constructive school
environment and culture.</p>
<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Compassionate</strong>&nbsp;facet focuses on the leadersâ€™
growth in social awareness and sensitivity to the needs of others, harnessing
the moral compass of every individual.</p>
<p>The TK student leader is the epitome of&nbsp;<strong>Competence</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>Constructiveness</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>Compassion</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Guiding Principles</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Opportunity for all</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reflective learning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Developmental and progressive</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Positive peer support and influence</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Empowerment through ownership</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Whole school approach through integration and collaboration</p>
</li>
</ul>
