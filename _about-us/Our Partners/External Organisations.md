<p><strong>Touch Young</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Arrow</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Student</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Leadership</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Programme</strong>
</p>
<p>Touch Young Arrow (TYA) has been collaborating with TK Student Council
since 2007. Volunteers from TYA mentored our student leaders to run various
social awareness campaign yearly. TYA also supports the Campus Changemakers
programme by providing platform for our students from the Principal Honour’s
List to contribute to meaningful community service.</p>
<p><strong>Counselling</strong>
</p>
<p>In TK, we believe that happy students make healthy students. We offer
comprehensive counselling services to help identify and address problems
that students face emotionally&nbsp; We have available school counsellor,
teacher counsellor and a professional counsellor from Touch Young Arrow
in attendance for face to face counselling, or counselling via electronic
means.</p>
<p><strong>St</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Hilda’s Community</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Services</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Community Outreach</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Project</strong>&nbsp;<strong>for</strong>&nbsp;<strong>the</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Elderly</strong>&nbsp;<strong>(COPE)</strong>
</p>
<p>TK collaborates with the South-East &nbsp;CDC, St Hilda’s Community Services
Centre and CityCab in the Community Outreach Project for the Elderly &nbsp;It
has enabled the school to reach out to residents in a cluster of three-room
flats at Kampong Arang that has a high percentage of elderly citizens.
TK students, together with volunteers from St Hilda’s Community Services
Centre, serve the residents on a regular basis.</p>
<p><strong>Institutes of Higher Learning</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Singapore Management University, Temasek Polytechnic, Singapore</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Polytechnic, Nanyang Polytechnic, Ngee Ann Polytechnic</strong>
</p>
<p>TK collaborates with industries and various Institutes of Higher Learning
(IHLs) to enhance student learning several key school programmes that are
borne out of this partnership include scholarship talks, teacher training,
educational dialogues with students, career guidance, self-directed learning
programme and character assessment through mock interviews.</p>
<p><strong>Citizens’ Consultative</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Committee</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Mountbatten CCC and Geylang Serai CCC</strong>
</p>
<p>The Mountbatten CCC and Geylang Serai CCC are close partners of TK. We
work closely with the CCCs on projects that benefit the community. Examples
include a Student Research project to develop the Mountbatten Trail, and
Project Serve which involves the cleaning and painting of houses for the
elderly and needy families in the Tanjong Rhu and Old Airport Road estates.</p>
<p><strong>Geylang East Polyclinic</strong>
</p>
<p>TK collaborates with the Geylang East Polyclinic to create opportunities
for TKsians to go on work attachment to gain insight into the running of
a public polyclinic.</p>
<p><strong>Association for People with Special Needs</strong>
</p>
<p>The Association for People with Special Needs (APSN) has collaborated
with us for various events. TK Band students have had the opportunity to
perform and teach clients from APSN how to play musical instruments. Our
students have also benefitted from horticulture classes conducted at APSN.</p>
<p><strong>Home-Fix Private</strong>&nbsp;<strong>Limited</strong>
</p>
<p>Together with St Hilda’s Community Services, Home-Fix has provided skilled-based
session on cleaning and painting, to better equip our students in their
effort to provide meaningful service to the needy family. This annual workshop
prepares our students for Project Serve, where students help in cleaning
and painting of homes in Kampong Arang and Tanjong Rhu estate.</p>
