undertakes. Confident, resilient and flexible, he is guided by sound values

<p><strong><u>Code Of Conduct</u></strong>
</p>
<p>EXPECTATIONS OF TKSIANS</p>
<p>Students observe a common set of expectations with regards to school practices
and classroom routines to maintain a positive learning environment in TK.</p>
<p>In School/General</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Help the subject teacher to begin and end lessons on time</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Consult the teacher with respect when in doubt</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exercise self-respect by being properly attired at all times</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Help keep the school clean</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Treat every person with respect</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create an inclusive learning environment by using language of care at
all time</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>In the Classroom</p>
<p>1. Start of Lesson</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Help keep the classroom clean at all times</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist in ensuring default classroom seating arrangement is neat</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Greet teacher/guest after the command signal by the class Chairman</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bring out the learning materials for the subject on your table</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>2. During lesson</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Quieten down quickly and give your attention when your teacher shows the
hand signal</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Raise your hand before posing questions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Stand when answering/posing question</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contribute your thoughts and viewpoints in class discussions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be attentive with no slouching on the table</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Refrain from visiting the toilet, unless urgent</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be at your best behaviour at all times</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>3. During lesson (Group work)</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Help arrange the class to get into group work seating arrangement upon
teacherâ€™s instructions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understand the objective and nature of group activity as well as ground
rules set</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform the assigned group roles for the group work activity and carry
out your responsibilities well</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>4. End of Lesson (last 5 minutes of period)</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Seek to understand the key points of your lesson</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Arrange classroom seating back to default mode (when there is group work)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Submit assignment by the row promptly (if necessary)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Refer to the homework/assignment board for work that require your attention</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Thank the teacher for the lesson</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proceed to prepare for the next lesson promptly</p>
</li>
</ul>
